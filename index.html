The Challenge

Our Oceans Need Us

Ghost Nets

Abandoned fishing nets drift through our oceans, trapping marine life and devastating ecosystems.

Marine Harm

Every year, countless sea creatures become entangled in discarded nets along our coasts.

500+ Years

Synthetic fishing nets take centuries to decompose, polluting waters for generations.

Our Answer

Transformation, Not Waste

Ghazl was born from a simple belief: ocean waste doesn't have to stay that way. Founded by Bahraini youth who grew up on these shores, we saw the problem washing up on our beaches and decided to create a solution.

We collect abandoned fishing nets from the Arabian Gulf, clean and process them, and transform them into meaningful products you can hold in your hands.

The Journey

From Net to New Life

Every Ghazl product tells a story of transformation.

01

Collection

Our team works with local fishermen and organizes coastal cleanups to recover abandoned nets from Bahrain's beaches.

02

Cleaning & Sorting

Each net is carefully cleaned of ocean debris, sorted by material type, and prepared for transformation.

03

Transformation

Using traditional weaving techniques and modern design, we reshape the nets into beautiful, functional products.

04

New Purpose

What was once ocean waste becomes a meaningful item—each product carries the memory of the sea.
